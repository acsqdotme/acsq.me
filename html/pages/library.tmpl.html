This collection is an incomplete venn diagram of books that I own or have read or both. Most books here are filled with varying amounts of knowledge with some really just being for entertainment; even with all of that, a small handful of them are truly epiphanic, and my life would be less meaningful without having read them.

I love talking about cool books. Feel free to email me about anything on here, and given that I've read it, I will share my thoughts and discuss.
